AnomX Safety Policy

AnomX is committed to protecting users from harmful and illegal content, including child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We strictly prohibit the sharing, uploading, or promotion of any form of CSAE content on our platform.

We moderate content using a combination of automated tools and manual review. Any violation of this policy will result in immediate account suspension and reporting to relevant authorities.

Reporting: If you see any content that may involve CSAE, please report it immediately to support@anomx.app or cyberwest.kz@gmail.com.

This policy complies with Google Play's requirements and international child safety standards.
